About Restaurent

Prash Production serves inventive vegetarian and non-vegetarian fusion dishes in a stunning ambiance. With seasonal specialties and elegant décor, it offers a delightful dining experience for guests looking to savor creative cuisine in a beautiful setting.

"Prash Production" is the culmination of a chef's global journey, blending diverse culinary influences into inventive fusion dishes. Rooted in family traditions and community values, the restaurant offers a welcoming space where guests can enjoy delicious meals made with locally sourced, sustainable ingredients. It's a celebration of creativity, heritage, and connection.

Our History

Specific

"Prash Production" tantalizes palates with its signature "Saffron-infused Butter Chicken" and aromatic "Biryani," marrying traditional Indian flavors with modern twists. Vegetarians indulge in the creamy "Butter Paneer," while the "Veg Biryani" offers a refreshing burst of seasonal ingredients. Each dish embodies culinary innovation, inviting guests on a flavorful journey through rich heritage and contemporary creativity.
